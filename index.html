    Introduction to Scout

    Scout is our product, a set of turn signals and brake light. The turn signals are activated from a control panel on the handlebars, while the brake light is triggered automatically using an accelerometer. Both of these features are completely wireless via BlueTooth, leaving no annoying wires prevent you from enjoying a smooth ride. Scout eliminates the need for hand signals which aren't known by everyone, and are difficult to see in dark situations.
